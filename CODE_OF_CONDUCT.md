# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. All participants are expected to treat each other with respect.

Standards
- Be kind and welcoming.
- Be respectful and considerate in your communications.
- Avoid demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior and language.

Enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers. Instances may be addressed by moderators at their discretion.

For the full text, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/
